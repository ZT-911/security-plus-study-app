Keyword: Data at Rest

Why it appears

Exam checks if you understand storage protection.

Trigger

Disk, database, backup → encryption required.

Example: Which control protects stored data?
Messer Insight: At rest = think encryption first, not firewall.

Trap: Clearing vs Secure Deletion

Why it appears

Students confuse deletion types.

Trigger

“Clearing” = recoverable → NOT secure.

Example: Company deletes files before selling laptop. Best answer is NOT clearing.
Messer Insight: Clearing ≠ secure. Purging or destruction is required.

Weak Point: Data in Use

Why it appears

Less obvious than rest/transit.

Trigger

RAM, processing → harder to protect.

Example: Data processed in memory → risk of exposure.
Messer Insight: In use is the hardest state to secure.

Trap: Classification Levels

Why it appears

Exam mixes impact levels.

Trigger

“Severe damage” → Restricted.

Example: Data leak causes major business damage → highest classification.
Messer Insight: Always map impact → classification level.
